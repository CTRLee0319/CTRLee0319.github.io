National Videogame Museum Website and Print design

Redesigning the printed marketing materials and website to attract visitors for the National Videogame Museum.

Client

National Videogame Museum

Year

2019

The National Videogame Museum is a museum that is located in Frisco, TX. The mission of the museum is to preserve and archive the history of video games by documenting all the related information first hand while providing an interactive experience for the visitors. To promote the membership program and attract visitors, a series of printed promotional materials and an overhauled website are created.

To stay relevant to the theme of video games, all the promotion materials played around the concept of video game evolution. The poster is inspired by The Beatle’s Abbie Road album cover, and depicting popular video game characters crossing the roads. The brochure mimics the design of an arcade cabinet and sees Mario runs through the different eras of video games. The website also follows a similar aesthetic, but gears towards a tidy, easy to read layout to provide information to the visitors.
