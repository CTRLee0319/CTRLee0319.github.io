High Speed Rail - An Infographic Overview

Raising awareness of High-Speed Rail in North America

Client

American Rail Club

Year

2019

High-Speed Rail has been inaugurated in Asia and Europe for decades, carrying thousands of passengers in safety and comfort that no other modes of transportation can offer. However, most Americans do not have any idea of what High-Speed Rail is capable of since the decline of passenger railroad after WWII. As High Speed Rail enters the public discourse in face of increasing congestion as well as concerns about climate change, this project is created to educate the public about the concepts of High-Speed Rail, the advantages, and comparing it to other modes of transportation such as driving or flying.
