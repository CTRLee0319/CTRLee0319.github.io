Who am I?

Hi. My name is Ryan.

I'm a highly versatile multimedia designer that is capable of solving communication problems via different media, and that includes everything from traditional print media, social media content all the way to motion and digital media, and even web design! 

Throughout the years in my career, I have worked in different industries and in different countries, and as so has developed a unique insight across different cultures that is also reflected on my works.

I am currently based in Hong Kong, and when I am not busy designing, I like to play sports, do photography and art, play video games and binge watch YouTube. (though I have to cut down the latter)
