National Videogame Museum Website and Brochure design

The National Videogame Museum is a museum that is located in Frisco, TX. The mission of the museum is to preserve and archive the history of video games by documenting all the related information first hand while providing an interactive experience for the visitors. To promote the membership program and attract visitors, a series of printed promotional materials and an overhauled website are created.
